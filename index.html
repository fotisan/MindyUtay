A unique perspective on professional stress and personal growth

Many of my clients are high-functioning professionals who feel unfulfilled, anxious, or stuck in repetitive patterns.

Before becoming a therapist, I practiced law in NYC. I understand firsthand the grueling hours, the pressure to perform, and the way a career can consume your identity. I pivoted to psychotherapy to help others navigate these exact challenges.

My approach is rooted in psychoanalytic and psychodynamic traditions. This means we don't just put a band-aid on symptoms; we look at the root causes, your history, and your relational patterns to create lasting change.

01

Career & Identity. helping you separate your self-worth from your productivity and find balance.

02

Relational Focus. Understanding how early relationships shape your current connections with partners and colleagues.

03

Addiction & Coping. As a CASAC, I specialize in helping those using substances to cope with stress.

I utilize Psychoanalytic and Psychodynamic Therapy alongside Relational approaches.

We explore how past experiences and unconscious drives influence your current behavior and choices.

2

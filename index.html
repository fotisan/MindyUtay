About Me

A unique perspective on professional stress

Before becoming a therapist, I spent 15 years as a lawyer in big law and finance. I have lived the high-pressure lifestyle and understand the toll it takes.

I realized that for many professionals, outward success often masks inner turmoil‚Äîanxiety, relationship disconnection, or a lingering sense of "is this it?". My transition to psychotherapy was driven by a desire to help people address these deeper emotional needs.

My approach is Psychodynamic and Relational. This means we don't just put a bandage on symptoms; we explore the root causes and patterns that keep you stuck, allowing for profound and lasting change.

Whether you are dealing with career burnout, relationship struggles, or simply feeling lost despite your achievements, I offer a safe, confidential space to do the work.
